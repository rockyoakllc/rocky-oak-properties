About us

We help make homes feel polished, personal, and ready to enjoy.

Rocky Oak Properties LLC is focused on thoughtful property improvements, design-forward details, and practical updates for homeowners across Middle Tennessee.

Family, home, and thoughtful details are at the heart of what we do.

Meet Ryan & Krystal

A hands-on team with construction roots and a passion for livable design.

Ryan and Krystal both grew up around construction. With fathers who were general contractors and handymen, they learned at a young age how much care, problem-solving, and craftsmanship goes into making a house feel complete.

Those early experiences shaped a shared passion for helping people turn a house into a home. Ryan brings a background in the tech industry and an engineering mindset, while Krystal has always carried a passion for affordable interior design and creating warm, approachable spaces.

Together, they balance each other out: practical planning, thoughtful design, clear communication, and a belief that beautiful spaces should feel personal, functional, and within reach.

Family is a big part of who they are. Ryan and Krystal care deeply for their two dogs, Ziggy and Marley, and hope to grow their family one day.

Who we are

A hands-on team with an eye for warm, livable design.

We believe the best homes are not just beautiful — they are functional, comfortable, and built around how people actually live. Our projects can include cabinet installation, painting, styling, wall treatments, finishing details, small repairs, and move-in support.

The Rocky Oak look is clean and modern with bohemian warmth: natural wood, soft neutrals, greenery, woven textures, simple lines, and intentional accents.

Our values

Simple process. Clear communication. Better spaces.

  • We listen first so the project supports your actual needs.
  • We recommend updates that balance style, cost, and practicality.
  • We care about the small finishing details that make a room feel done.
